How to buy

1. Create and use your CurioWallet account to manage your cards.

2. Send ethereum from your CurioWallet to one of the vending machine addresses.

3. View your new cards in the Gallery and your CurioWallet.

Check out the Curio Marketplace to trade your cards.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is Curio Cards?

Answer

Curio Card's mission is to create a platform for artists and collectors that finally benefits artists. Until now it's not been possible to sell digital artwork without massive fees, theft, or restrictive DRM.
Curio is changing that.

Now you can own rare and collectible digital artwork, and interact with it in amazing new ways, all built on proven blockchain technology.

We encourage you to join our Discord channel to keep up-to-date, share your ideas, and meet the Curio community.


Click Here to Join!

Q: I'm an artist, how can I make my own cards?

Answer

Welcome to Curio Cards, we are thrilled you found this project. We are featuring select artists currently, with plans to massively open up the platform to everyone as we scale up.

Please let us know about your work and your interest by submitting it on the Community Site, and joining our Discord channel to let us know about yourself.

Q: How can I get sold out cards? What does it mean to own a sold out card?

Answer

For cards that are sold out, the total supply that will ever exist is now in the hands of individual owners. Those cards are no longer available from the Curio Cards site artists, and the only way to get them is to trade with people who currently own those cards.

Q: What wallets are supported?

Answer

As long as it's a wallet that lets you control the private keys, you will recieve your Curio Cards.
But you won't be able to see or manage them unless you use CurioWallet. Devices like the Trezor and Ledger S nano are also compatable with CurioWallet, allowing you to easily store your Curio Cards in a secure hardware wallet.

CurioWallet is mobile compatable, and we will be adding native iOS and Android apps.

Don't use ethereum wallets from exchanges when buying cards. You will not recieve your cards since you do not control the private keys.
